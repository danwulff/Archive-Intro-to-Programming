Voting!!

Welcome to the world of voting. Corporations essentially own the elections, so democracy at this point is more or less a sham. If you do want to participate as a hopeful citizen, you have three options: go to a voting booth near you, drop an envelope in a dropbox, or mail your vote. Good luck getting anything done or having your vote actually count.

This may not be as cynical as if you were of voting age, because you are too young to handle the truth. At this point, it is all about the founding fathers and the mechanics of democracy that fuel our great nation.
